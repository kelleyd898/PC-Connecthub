Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PC-ConnectHub ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit our educational reference website. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We are a strictly informational resource. To ensure your privacy:

  • We do not require user registration or accounts.
  • We do not ask for names, phone numbers, or physical addresses.
  • We do not install software on your computer.
  • We do not request remote access to your device.

2. Log Data and Analytics

Like most websites, we may collect non-personal technical data transmitted by your browser, such as your IP address, browser type, and referring pages. This data is used solely for website performance analysis and security monitoring.

3. Cookies

We may use basic session cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. We do not use cookies to track your behavior across other websites or to build personal profiles.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to official manufacturer websites (e.g., HP, Canon). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to review their policies when you leave our site.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the contact information provided on our website.
